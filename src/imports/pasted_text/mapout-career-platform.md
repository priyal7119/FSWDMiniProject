
Design a **modern professional student career platform UI** called:

MAPOUT – AI Career Development Platform

The platform helps students:

• Build resumes
• Identify skill gaps
• Plan career paths
• Discover projects
• Learn research writing
• Prepare for interviews

The website should look like a **modern startup-level product used by students**, while remaining **simple enough for a second-year engineering student to implement using React or basic frontend tools**.

The design must be:

• visually appealing
• colorful but balanced
• calm and professional
• aesthetic and modern
• clean and structured
• aligned and systematic

Avoid chaotic or overly playful design.
